# Affiliate Disclosure

**Last updated: 18 June 2026**

RoomNabs is reader-supported. Some of the links on this website are affiliate
links, which means we may earn a commission if you click through and make a
purchase — at no extra cost to you.

**As an Amazon Associate, we earn from qualifying purchases.**

We also participate in other affiliate programmes and may earn commissions from
qualifying purchases made through links to those retailers.

## What this means

- When you click a link to a retailer (such as Amazon) and buy a product, we may
  receive a small commission.
- This commission comes out of the retailer's margin. It does **not** increase
  the price you pay.
- Earning a commission never changes which products we recommend or what we say
  about them.

## Our promise on recommendations

The commission we may earn does not influence our content. We choose and describe
products based on specifications, value, and suitability for small homes and
rentals — not on which retailer pays the most. Where we state a price, it is an
approximate figure that can change at any time; always check the current price on
the retailer's site before buying.

## Questions

If you have any questions about our use of affiliate links, please contact us via
our contact page.
