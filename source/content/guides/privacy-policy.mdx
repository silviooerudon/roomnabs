# Privacy Policy

**Last updated: 18 June 2026**

This Privacy Policy explains how RoomNabs ("we", "us", "our") collects, uses, and
protects information when you visit roomnabs.com (the "Site"). We are committed to
protecting your privacy and handling your data in line with the EU General Data
Protection Regulation (GDPR) and the Irish Data Protection Act 2018.

## Who we are

RoomNabs is a home and tech product recommendation website. For any privacy-
related queries, you can reach us through our contact page.

## What information we collect

- **Information you provide:** If you subscribe to our newsletter, we collect the
  email address you submit.
- **Information collected automatically:** Like most websites, we may collect
  standard technical data such as your browser type, device, approximate location
  (derived from IP), and how you use the Site, through cookies and analytics
  tools.

We do not ask for or store sensitive personal information.

## How we use your information

We use the information we collect to:

- Send you our newsletter, if you have subscribed
- Understand how visitors use the Site so we can improve it
- Maintain the security and functionality of the Site

## Cookies

The Site may use cookies and similar technologies for analytics and basic
functionality. You can control or disable cookies through your browser settings.
Where required, we will ask for your consent before setting non-essential cookies.

## Analytics

We may use third-party analytics services to understand Site usage. These
services may set their own cookies and process data according to their own privacy
policies.

## Affiliate links

The Site contains affiliate links. When you click them, the destination retailer
(such as Amazon) may collect information according to their own privacy policy. We
do not control and are not responsible for the privacy practices of third-party
sites. Please see our Affiliate Disclosure for more detail.

## How we share your information

We do not sell your personal information. We may share data with service providers
(such as email and analytics providers) only as needed to operate the Site, and
with authorities where required by law.

## Your rights under GDPR

You have the right to access, correct, or delete your personal data, to object to
or restrict its processing, and to data portability. To exercise any of these
rights, or to unsubscribe from our newsletter, contact us through our contact
page or use the unsubscribe link in any newsletter email.

## Data retention

We keep your information only for as long as necessary for the purposes described
in this policy, or as required by law.

## Children

The Site is not directed at children under 16, and we do not knowingly collect
their personal data.

## Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The "last updated" date at
the top reflects the most recent changes.

## Contact

For any questions about this Privacy Policy or your data, please contact us
through our contact page.
